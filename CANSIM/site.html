    Debt Analytics & Metrics:

    Most of the households around the developed countries are highly leveraged. Are these leverage ratios optimal in sense that minimize the cost of capital for households without acquiring a high possibility of default under an economic downturn? Or especially if interest rates start to rise again as more debt means higher debt service ratios?

    In this work using the data from Cansim , the optimal leverage dynamics are compared to the current leverage dynamics and a statistical assessment is conducted to see if the new indicator related to the debt service ratio is appropriately reflecting an early warning indicator. The methodology, the data inputs and some results are discussed in the remainder of this essay.
