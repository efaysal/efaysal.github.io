    Introduction

    Predicting the aggregate risk emerging in stock markets is of paramount importance. I investigate both theoretically and empirically information contagion and systemic risk in stocks markets, when investors are sensitive to news about the stocks, bad news about a number of stocks can adversely spill over to other stocks. We study this information contagion in a number of stock mrkets. Systemic risk is defined as the probability of joint movement in the stock market prices.
