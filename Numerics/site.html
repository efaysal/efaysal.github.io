    Digital Analytics & Metrics:

    Our Equity Research uses a multi-layered framework: Mathematical financial modeling to build an analytical assessment of a financial asset or portfolio. Data related to corporation, sector, industry and hedge fund equity portfolio construction have been integrated to provide key components driving financial asset or portfolio performance.

    Numerics:

    The featured numerical results used daily historical prices from a given prior date to a given posterior date. Often we use data from a quarter to make comparisons and evaluate trends.

    For the number of times a stock has been included in hedge fund equity portfolios and their associated capitalization (at market close 2016-04-15), click here. This is mainly normative, it may happen that the hedge fund manager changed the portfolio's composition of the published 13F holdings before this calculation.
