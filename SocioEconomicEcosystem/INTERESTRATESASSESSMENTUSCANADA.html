Interest Rates

Insights From Historical Data

Using Mathematical Modeling & Machine Learning Tools

Faysal.El.Khettabi@gmail.com

    Introduction:

    In many countries operating under Well-designed central Banks, the interest rates are mainly determined by the real economical conditions. One of the most influential economics tool the central Bank has under its exclusive control is the ability to regulate these interest rates indirectly via its monetary policy. The central Bank carries out monetary policy by influencing specially short-term interest rates. It does this by raising and lowering the target for the overnight rate(Canada) or discount rate(USA) .

    An important question is how much trust can be placed on such "target for the overnight rate" or "discount rate" to influence really interest rates in an economy?

    Our quantitative analysis uses a set of mathematical models to evaluate the historical interest rates in public databases for patterns that help to answer the question in terms of numerical values. These numerical values are presented into a form guided by this statement "Translating and analyzing big data using charts, graphics, and images is becoming more and more necessary for decision-making." promoted by the Association of Public Data Users.

    We provide:
    • Interactive Charting features the temporal dynamics of the considered interest rates in this work and to facilitate their comparisons. To find out more about the temporal dynamic of a specific interest rate, a double click on its name in the legend creates its associated curve and then add up for multiple comparisons.

    • Interest Rates Network is the results of our mathematical modeling and subsequent computation to study the numerical behavior of these interest rates. The obtained numerical values are used to explore and gain new insights into the impact of the temporal decision that determined them and to estimate and highlight specific interconnection levels between them. If there exist groups of interest rates highly interconnected but with few links to other interest rates. This kind of structure carry out much information about the network to answer our question quantitatively.

    • Random Walks on Interest Rates Network, by considering the interest rates network as a Graph (discrete mathematics), the theory of random walk on this graph help to extract basic properties determined by the spectrum of the graph as its community structures and stationary distribution.

    Interpretation of stationary distribution values

    More the stationary distribution value of a given interest rate is higher more this interest rate is affecting significantly the values of the considered interest rates. The interest rates in the lowest stationary distribution class have more difficulty to influence significantly other interest rates values than the interest rates in the highest stationary distribution class.
    • In the case of USA, the medium term interest rates( 3 months, 6 months, 1 to 3 years) are more influence-able than the effective Federal Funds Rate(FF), FF is moderately influencing other interest rates. The second surprising fact, the interest rates related to Moody's AAA/BAA Bond are blunt and are mathematically just a derivation of the other interest rates.

    • While in Canada, the Bank Rate and Target Rate have less-prominent role to influence significantly the interest rates than the medium term interest rates, one year to 5 years.
